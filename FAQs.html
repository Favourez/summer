Frequently Asked Questions

The Graduate Portal is a platform designed to help recent graduates with job searching, social networking, and accessing relevant articles and resources.

You can update your profile by navigating to the Profile section from the dashboard and making changes to your information.

You can apply for jobs by visiting the Job Search section, where you can browse available positions and submit your applications directly.
